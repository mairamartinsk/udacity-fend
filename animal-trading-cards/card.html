Dainty Green Tree Frog

Also known as Graceful Tree Frog, this little frog can sometimes be found sleeping inside lilies.

  • Scientific Name: Litoria gracilenta
  • Average Length: 45 millimeters (1.8 in)
  • Distribution: Northeastern coast of Australia
  • Habitat: Open forests, woodlands and suburban gardens

Bright green with yellow slides, belly and feet. Eyes are bright orange. It is usually seen after heavy rain during spring and summer. It breeds in flooded grassland and small ponds and the males form noisy call choruses during breeding season.
